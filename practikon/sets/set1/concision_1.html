Comments on Response Options

Problem Text

I plan to receive my B.S. in Computer Science from Carnegie Mellon, the university that is leading in Computer Science, which has contributed to the quality of my education and my value of teamwork.

Option A

“a leader in Computer Science,”

This option is the most concise and places more emphasis on the university’s position in general. This would be appropriate for applications in the job industry and for people more familiar with the University.

Option B

“a leading university in Computer Science,”

This option is also very concise, allowing the emphasis to be on the university’s position in the academic world. This would work better for graduate school applications and academic audiences in general.

Option C

“the number one university in the field of Computer Science,”

This option is not as concise as the first two, but makes explicit the university’s position in rankings; it would be more appropriate if your purpose is to impress the readers.
