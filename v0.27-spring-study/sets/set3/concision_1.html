In the challenges that follow, try to consider the options presented so that the developers can make the most effective case for their audience.

Problem Text

Squlite3 Gem:
While developing and testing our system, we used the SQlite 3 gem. Although this is the most current and latest version, we can continue to update it and make more updated versions.

Option A

“the leading university in Computer science, “

This option cuts out the most unnecessary information which allows it to place emphasis on university’s title.

Option B

“the number one university in Computer Science,”

This option is also concise, but adds information to the university’s title; it would sound good if your purpose is to place emphasis on the title.

Option C

“the university that is number one in Computer Science,”

This option adds to the title like in the second option, but it also keeps unnecessary information, which makes it less concise and would only benefit an audience placing less emphasis on the title.
